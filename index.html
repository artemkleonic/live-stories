Translation manifesto

A story is not a suitcase. It cannot simply be moved from one language to another.

Voice first

The rhythm, irony and silence of the author matter as much as the meaning of the words.

No fake shelves

If a story is not ready in a language, it stays hidden instead of pretending.

Slow is honest

Translations will appear one by one, because literature should not be rushed through a machine.
